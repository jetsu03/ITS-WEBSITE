Events Calendar 2025

ProtoNova 2025

An interdisciplinary student development and prototyping event where teams of students are tasked with developing and presenting a prototype for the given problem statement. The event aims to foster innovation and critical real-world problem solving skills among students. It also aims to encourage interdisciplinary collaboration among students.

CryptoSphere

An event exploring the key concepts of cryptography and emphasizing its role in securing modern digital communication and encryption/decryption systems. The event also expands on the usage of cryptography in blockchain and cybersecurity. The event aims to help participants develop a foundational understanding of cryptography and its importance in the present and the future. It also aims to encourage participants to contribute to the world of cryptography.

GitCraft

A seminar to explore Git, followed by an interactive challenge where participants replicate and enhance an existing project. The event concludes with a questioning round where participants defend the modifications and solutions. The event is aimed at sharpening the participants' Git skills, from basic commands to advanced project collaboration techniques. It also allows participants to learn hands-on and gain a deep understanding of version control systems.

IEEE Information Theory Society

Advancing the theoretical and applied aspects of information technology.
