BE AN IEEE MEMBER

1

Visit ieee.org and click on "JOIN IEEE"

2

Scroll down, and choose "Join as a student"

3

Create an account, and fill in your credentials

4

Once you're logged in, fill in your contact and profile information (add the college's address), and click on "Continue"

5

Fill out Education Information. Once Country and State have been filled out, click on "Search" and look up BMS Institute of Technology.

6

Fill out the Additional Information field, and click "continue".

7

Under Membership Application, search for "Information Theory Society". Click on the Societies tab and click on "Information Theory Society Membership".

8

Select "Essential" under the membership type and click on ADD ITEM(S). Click Continue.

9

Your cart should look something like this.

10

Select your preferred mode of payment and complete your registration. You're done!
